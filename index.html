How GridPeer works
Metering. Financing. Ownership. The platform solar adoption was always missing.

GridPeer is a complete metering, financing, and registration platform. We finance new solar systems for communities who couldn't afford one, sub-meter tenants across estates, and handle every compliance step under the Electricity Act 2023. Every household we bring online displaces a generator. At scale, that is a structural shift in Nigeria's emissions baseline.

Per-line metering The Hub measures every kilowatt-hour flowing to each connected neighbour. No estimates. No disputes.
Prepaid wallet via WhatsApp or USSD No smartphone needed. Supply pauses automatically when balance hits zero. No manual intervention.
Physical isolation and real-time safety Every line gets its own breaker, surge protection, and earthing. NEMSA-certified contractors only. No exceptions.

The Hardware
GridPeer Hub. The only device you need.

Off-the-shelf components. Built-in GSM. Designed to be installed in 90 minutes and run for years without attention.

Built-in GSM. No host WiFi required.The Hub connects independently on any Nigerian mobile network. The host's personal internet is never involved.
Per-line kilowatt-hour metering.Exact measurement of power flowing to each neighbour. Supply cuts automatically when their wallet balance hits zero.
Physical isolation. Every single line.Dedicated breaker, surge protection, and earthing at the distribution board. Not tapped from household wiring. NEMSA-certified install only.
Real-time fault detection.Temperature and current monitoring on every line. Alerts the owner before a fault becomes a fire. Second layer on top of physical isolation.
NERC registration handled automatically.The Hub registers under the Electricity Act 2023 sub-100 kW framework. Compliance is built in, not bolted on.
